About Us

The Isa Mundo (One World) Foundation was born from a dream and a vision that in the future healthy families and communities will populate the world. By sharing our skills and the world's resources in a sustainable manner, we can ensure that no community is left behind.

It would take a global effort and a commitment from each and every one of us in order to attain this goal. There is a saying that if one has passion, then one has something to contribute. So it is not about asking, "What should I do?" It is about asking, "What is my passion?"

Let us all pledge to be part of the solution because "wherever we turn, we can always find someone who needs us."
